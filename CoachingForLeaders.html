Coaching for Leaders

Leading by Design

Over eight weeks, you’ll gain a deeper understanding of your leadership style—how you show up in calm and high-pressure moments—and develop strategies to lead with empathy, clarity, and impact. You'll learn how to rally your team, navigate challenges with confidence, and bring out the best in others (and yourself).

Additional Elements

Weekly Schedule

Over the course of eight weeks, this program will help you lead with empathy, clarity, and confidence.

Week 1
Understanding Your Leadership Profile
We begin with a skills and preferences matrix, alongside a personality test, to uncover your natural leadership style.
Week 2
Strengths and Growth Areas
Dive into your leadership strengths and areas for development, setting a foundation for intentional growth.
Week 3
Leading Under Pressure
Explore how you respond to stress and develop strategies to maintain composure and clarity.
Week 4
Emotional Intelligence and Empathy
Learn techniques to build emotional intelligence, enhance communication, and foster team trust.
Week 5
Decision-Making and Influence
Master the art of making confident decisions and influencing others effectively.
Week 6
Conflict Resolution and Feedback
Develop skills to navigate difficult conversations and give/receive constructive feedback.
Week 7
Building High-Performing Teams
Learn how to foster a culture of collaboration, motivation, and accountability.
Week 8
Creating Your Leadership Action Plan
Bring everything together in a personalized leadership roadmap for sustained growth.

Becoming a better leader is a lifelong journey. The best leaders I've worked with are continuously evolving, adapting, and refining their approach. As you put these strategies into action, you’ll build greater clarity, confidence, and impact in your career. If you’re curious about my approach or want to explore more ways to grow, learn more about me and let's connect!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if leadership coaching is right for me?

If you’re looking to sharpen your leadership skills, improve decision-making, or navigate challenges with confidence, coaching can help.

It’s for both experienced leaders and new ones who want to lead with clarity and impact.

What kind of leaders do you coach?

I work with executives, managers, and high-potential leaders in design, product, and tech.

Whether you're stepping into leadership or refining your approach, coaching is tailored to your challenges.

I also help leadership teams formulate and communicate strategy so that they are ready for what's next for their business. Check out team workshops for more info on how I help leadership teams think creatively, collaborate effectively, and solve problems with confidence.

How is coaching different from mentorship?

Coaching helps you uncover your own answers through strategic questioning and reflection. Unlike mentorship, which shares personal experience and is advice-based, coaching empowers you to shape your own leadership style and strategies.

I’m here to hold up a mirror—helping you reflect on where you’ve been, where you’re going, and what’s possible. You’ll gain fresh perspectives, move forward with greater clarity, and take meaningful steps toward your goals.

What results can I expect from coaching?

The results can be life-changing—if you're ready to put in the work! Once you’re willing to step outside your comfort zone and challenge your blind spots, you’ll develop sharper decision-making skills, stronger communication, and a clearer leadership vision.

You’ll gain strategies to navigate conflict, drive alignment, and create lasting impact in your organization. Think of me as your co-pilot, helping you tackle tough situations and equipping you with tools to handle challenges you haven’t faced before.

How often should I do coaching sessions?

I offer biweekly or monthly sessions, allowing you time to apply insights between meetings. We’ll tailor a schedule that fits your goals and workload.

If you're navigating a big career change or facing tough situations at work, I also offer weekly sessions with text check-ins between meetings to keep you on track.
