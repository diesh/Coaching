Privacy Policy

Privacy has never been more important (or threatened). We respect yours.

This policy applies to all coaching services provided by Gagan Diesh, operating under Z1 DesignStamp Creative Group Inc. ("DesignStamp"). DesignStamp values your privacy as much as we value our own. We never sell, lend, or otherwise allow a third party to use any information we may collect from you.

User-Focused Data Handling

Our business relies on learning about our coaching clients and the professional challenges they wish to solve. We never share personal data with anyone without their explicit permission.

Contact Forms and Personal Information

When you reach out via our contact or intake forms, we collect the personal information you provide, such as your name, email address, and any details regarding your coaching needs. This information is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries.
  • Provide and personalize our coaching services.
  • Manage our professional relationship.

You may decline further email communication from us at any time. We do not share your personal information with other companies or third parties unless compelled by law or court order.

Analytics and Cookies

To improve the usability and navigability of our website, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to measure traffic and engagement. This helps us understand how visitors interact with the site.

What we collect via Analytics:

  • Referring Links: How you found our site.
  • IP Addresses: To understand geographic trends.
  • System Information: Including web browser versions, OS, and device types to ensure the site displays correctly.
